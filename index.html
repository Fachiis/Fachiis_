Chocolate cake recipe

"Here is a fast and easy version of the classic Chocolate Cake recipe.Enjoy!"

DifficultyPreparationCookingTotal time
Easy10 min25 min35 min

Ingredients

Black chocolate: 200g

Sugar: 150g

Butter: 200g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80g

Instructions

1

Melt the chocolate and the butter together

2

Mix the sugar, egg and the flour

3

Mix everything to get a smooth dough

4

Bake in the oven
