Website for a cake recipe

I created this web application for ease of ordering food, drinks and a lot more items by the client customers.
The Objectives: Gain visibility for food and drinks lovers, display the food and drinks to users that visit the site for trolling, satisfaction and being able to place an order directly to the business owner account via whatsapp immediately.

This project is simple and easy to flow but educative and efficient for those wanting to build a food web application for ease of customer satisfaction and orders. My client was very happy with the outcome of the project.

Technologies: HTML, CSS, W3CSS, JavaScript, Django, Python, MySQL, Git, WhoGoHost
